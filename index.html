Cardio Monitoring

The Cardio Monitoring department has excellent facilities for clinical care and fully developed facilities for adult and pediatric interventions including coronary interventions, valvuloplasty, device closure of congenital shunts and electrophysiology services.

Medical Treatment

Department of Medical Treatment is one of the pivotal departments of AV Hospital. It has developed from being only general medicine which includes: Critical care and Emergency Medicine, Endoscopies, Respiratory Medicine, and HIV medicine, Diabetology, Nephrology, Rheumatology.

Casualty

Casualty is the main department. In this department 24 hours service will be provided. In casualty department on primary basis critically ill patients are treated. Patients are treated after emergency patient are admitted in general ward & ICU some patient are treated on OPD basis.

Nursing

Nursing service is a devoted profession in the service to people which provides curative, preventive, and primitive health services to individual, family, and community. In nursing services, the nurse works with the members of allied disciples such as dietetics, medical social service, pharmacy etc.

Regular Case

The regular case service refers to the general medical services offered to patients who require routine care, such as for common illnesses and injuries.The regular case service may be provided by a range of healthcare professionals, including primary care physicians, specialists, nurses, and other support staff.

Read More

Serious Case

The serious case service refers to the specialized medical care provided to patients with complex or life-threatening medical conditions.The serious case service requires highly trained healthcare professionals, such as critical care physicians, oncologists, neurologists, and other specialists who work together to provide multidisciplinary care to patients.

Read More

Emergency Case

The emergency case service of refers to the urgent medical care provided to patients who require immediate attention due to a sudden illness, injury, or medical condition that could be life-threatening if not treated promptly.The focus is on providing timely, life-saving interventions and stabilizing patients before transferring them to the appropriate level of care.
